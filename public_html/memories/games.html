Noah's Childhood Games

Nostalgic games from my childhood. Mostly games available online from websites like Armor games or Kongregate, although there are a couple that were downloadable applications only.

Insaniquarium Deluxe

Feed fish, collect coins, get better fish! Defeat Aliens! It's a vibe. Available on Steam.

Pocket Tanks

Play with friends, fight tanks! Available as a downloadable application.

How to Raise a Dragon

Locked up by a wizard as a baby dragon, pick an element and grow to adulthood to take revenge!

Territory War

Control stick-figures and fight bots or eachother to gain territory! This is the game that taught me how to spell territory!

Age of War I (or II)

The first 2D RTS game I played! Advance through the ages and defeat the enemy!

Dino Run

Run to score points, find eggs, hats and escape extinction! Outrun the darkness!

Fancy Pants Adventures

Slide through the levels with the fanciest of pants on!

Crush the Castle I (or II)

Take revenge on the kingdom after being refused by the King for something (I don't remember what, lol)! But CRUSH THOSE CASTLES!

Interactive Buddy

Oh, the poor little guy. We had some great times though, didn't we?

Shopping Cart Hero I (or II, or III, or didn't they make 4 of these?)

An adrenaline junkie, shopping cart obsessor, and... a father? Ride those shopping carts at all costs!

Monster Evolution

Eat people and avoid the popo to evolve into an even bigger and badder monster!

Raft Wars

Babies, pirates, guns, and treasure! What more could you ask for in a game?

Sydney Shark

Remember the typing class they made me take because I was absent on the day to test out? I'd finish my assignments in 5 minutes and play this the rest of the time! What a blast!

Haunt the House I (or II)

Possess objects and haunt the residents! Don't go overboard or they won't be scared anymore!

Staggy the Boy Scout Slayer I (or II)

Boy Scouts? In MY forest?! I don't think so!

Bubble Tanks I (or II, or III)

Murder other bubbles to make make your bubble tank bigger and better! Become the best bubble tank!

Bunni How we first met

Be the king of bunni island! Unlock new islands, bunni helpers! And... I really don't remember what the overall goal was but I remember having fun!

SPORE

Starting from humble beginnings, eat or befriend other species, and emerge as the dominant species on your planet!

Kitten Cannon

Upgrade your cannon to launch your kitten farther each time!

Toss the Turtle I (or II)

Launch the turtle from a cannon! Navigate towards bombs to go even farther! (Disclaimer: Not recommended for turtle-lovers)

Stick War

Another RTS where you fight stick people and upgrade them! I remember not being very good but it was fun!

Pacxon

Use Pacman to make the area smaller and trap the ghosts!

Pajama Sam

DI remember spending a LOT of time eating candy in the first room at the party lol -- it looked good!

Zoombinis

Solve puzzles to sacrifice the least number of Zoombinis on a perilous journey to somewhere else! The funnest part was designing them in the beginning :)
